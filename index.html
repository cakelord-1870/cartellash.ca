Finally, a makeup remover that’s gentle, powerful, yet totally lash-safe. Our innovative, oil-free formula feels like water, yet emulsifies on contact to completely dissolve even long-wear eye makeup. No rubbing, no tugging and, best of all, no damage to your lashes! And unlike other removers, there’s no greasy residue. Formulated with fatty acids to dissolve makeup and prevent moisture loss, Breakdown works so well, you’ll want to use it even when you’re not wearing lashes.

Size: 100ml / 3.38 fl oz

HOW TO USE:

Saturate Clean Swoop Brush with Breakdown, gently working into the lash line and around the eye area to dissolve makeup. Wet brush with water and run over lashes to rinse. Pat dry.
